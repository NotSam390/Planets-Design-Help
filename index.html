Unmineable terrain feature. Continuously shoots SO₂ gas particles. Full screen flash during eruptions.

Jupiter
Massive, fills 30% of the sky (~20° apparent). Highly detailed color bands and Great Red Spot. Provides night illumination.

05 — Titan

Dense Atmosphere & Terrain
Gen Type
Tholin dune fields, rolling plains, water-ice mountains
Methane Seas
50-300 block wide dark orange-black fluid bodies with connected river channels
Custom Blocks
modpack:liquid_methane

Viscous semi-opaque orange-brown fluid. Causes extreme freezing damage without appropriate thermal player suits.

modpack:tholin_buildup

Visual residue block placed over time onto multiblock machines by the Drowning Spire. Cleaned with a Wrench.

Skybox (Orange Soup)
Atmosphere Haze
#e6b800 (Uniform dense golden-yellow haze). Heaviest fog in pack. Max visibility 80 blocks.
Visibility
Sun disc, stars, and Saturn are completely hidden (only faint light patches or thin ring streaks bleed through at night).

Tiger Stripes & Geysers
Gen Type
Flat ice plains (95% at Y=4)
Tiger Stripes
4-6 linear parallel chasms filled with geothermal active blocks
Custom Blocks
modpack:tiger_stripe

Fractured core block. Fires a massive white particle column plume every 90 seconds (shoots 20-40 blocks high). Required base position for Geyser Cradle legs.

07 — Triton

Nitrogen Plains
Gen Type
Pale pinkish-white flat nitrogen ice plains
Dark Streaks
Long organic windblown soot deposits generated downwind from geysers. Harvestable for carbon dust.
Custom Blocks
modpack:nitrogen_geyser

Low pressure cryo-vent. Periodically emits soft, wind-drifting nitrogen wisps.

modpack:cantaloupe_ice

Unique dimpled rind pattern structural ice biome variant.

Skybox (Retrograde Motion)
Neptune View
Vivid azure blue sphere (15° size). Rises in the WEST and sets in the EAST due to retrograde planetary orbit rule.
Ambient Depth
Darkest baseline sky in pack. Sun is reduced to a bright pinpoint star. Sky displays dense Milky Way star textures.

08 — Ariel

Chasm Gorges
Gen Type
Extremely deep canyon fault networks cutting through rolling lavender-tinted ice plains
Canyon Floors
Depressions down to Y=-60. Accumulates thick CO₂ frost fields and rare subsurface methane clathrates.
Custom Blocks
modpack:methane_clathrate

Ore block containing trapped crystalline bubbles. Tagged as clathrate_ore; needed for Canyon Teeth logic.

modpack:ariel_canyon_wall

Layered ice-rock strata blocks. Built-in verona_rupes tag validation check.

Skybox
Uranus View
Serene featureless pale cyan-green sphere (18° size). Rendered with pole-on spin orientation (98° axial tilt). Thin dark rings.
Lighting
Canyon deep zones clamp lighting values (Y<0 applies light reduction penalty of 2).

09 — Charon

Polar Tholin Cap
Gen Type
Muted space-weathered dark grey plains contrasted by a giant red south pole biome
Tholin Cap
Covers ~35% of the total world space. Deep red organic crust. Base home for the Red Altar.
Custom Blocks
modpack:charon_tholin

Granular red-orange block. Carries polar_tholin code tag mapping criteria.

Skybox (Mutual Tidal Lock)
Pluto View
Massive 25° sphere showing tan heart plains (Tombaugh Regio). Locked completely stationary. Only renders on Pluto-facing coordinates. The opposite hemisphere is total void.

10 — Miranda

Geological Chaos & Verona Rupes
Gen Type
Shattered upthrust ice plates (35% chaotic terrain layouts)
Verona Rupes
Monolithic drop cliff. Suddenly shifts from surface Y=20 down to chasm depths of Y=-80. An 80+ block sheer drops vertical void where Verona Harrow multi is built.
Custom Blocks
modpack:verona_wall

Alternating layered cliff strata pattern block. Interacts with Verona Harrow structure checks.

modpack:carbonaceous_chondrite

Dark rock filled with metallic inclusions. Drops chunk plus rare PGM traces.

Skybox
Uranus View
Large 24° sphere. Ring profile slightly more visible here than from Ariel. Cool blue shadow effects cast along cliffs.

11 — Proteus

Lumpy Non-Spherical World
Gen Type
Ultra-irregular lumpy shape surface profile. No flat expanses. Sharp ridges and constant bumpy drops.
Equator Band
Z-aligned ring plane settling zone. Coated with ring dust block layers.
Custom Blocks
modpack:proteus_dark

Ultra-low albedo dark surface block tag holder. Mimics black velvet by swallowing almost all light reflections.

modpack:ring_dust

Thin powder cover layout. Required ecosystem block variable for Void Bloom execution paths.

Skybox (Dynamic Ring Plane)
Neptune View
Prograde fast orbit (1.12 Sol cycles). Neptune actively rises in East and sets in West at a massive 20° scale.
Ring Line
Renders a subtle, thin light-grey diagonal streak stretching directly across the sky field.

12A — Eris

Endgame Methane Snow Desert
Gen Type
Flawless white landscape desert (95% flat at Y=4). Methane snow completely fills and buries all default impact crater features.
Custom Blocks
modpack:methane_snow

Brilliant high-albedo white surface layer with trace pink organic hues.

modpack:noble_gas_pocket_eris

Identical to multi-ice mix but displays occasional purple shimmers. Deep gen element, extremely rare.

Skybox (The Scattered Throne)
Deep Space
Absolute black vacuum. The Sun is rendered identically to standard background stars, differentiated only by its high point intensity.
Dysnomia Link
When endgame Throne structures match cross-body link states, a solid vector particle line renders between Dysnomia's position and the zenith.

12B — Dysnomia

Carbonate Satellite
Gen Type
Small, irregular dark carbonaceous chunk moon tracking Eris. Constant slight elevation ridges. No craters.
Custom Blocks
modpack:dysnomia_carbonate

Very dark brown-grey structural rock vein texture. Refined by the Scattered Throne into exotic ceramic compounds.

Skybox (High Contrast Mirror)
Eris Shine
Hangs locked in sky as a blinding 15° pure white sphere. Due to methane snow albedo tracking, it mirrors sunlight powerfully, casting strong bright white illumination over Dysnomia's dark brown surface floor.
